Cheyenne Mountain Zoo Under Investigation After Rare Albino Sloth "Heidi" Dies Under "Mysterious Circumstances"

Miles Blumhardt
Fort Collins Coloradoan

November 15, 2024 Cheyenne Mountain Zoo is facing an ongoing investigation following the sudden and unexplained death of a rare albino sloth named Heidi. The sloth, known for her striking white fur and distinct pink eyes, passed away last week, leaving both zoo officials and animal conservationists baffled and concerned. Heidi, one of the few albino sloths in captivity, was a popular attraction at the zoo and her death has sparked widespread outcry from animal lovers and calls for transparency from the institution.

The mysterious circumstances surrounding Heidi's death have prompted an inquiry by the Colorado Parks and Wildlife Department (CPW), as well as a separate investigation by animal welfare groups. The case has drawn attention due to the rarity of albino sloths a condition that occurs in fewer than 1 in 10,000 sloths in the wild and the high level of care required for such a unique animal.

Heidi, a two-toed sloth, had been residing at Cheyenne Mountain Zoo for over three years. She was part of the zoos conservation efforts to protect and raise awareness about sloth populations in the wild, particularly the endangered species in Central and South America. Heidi's albinism made her even more of an anomaly, garnering significant attention from visitors who were mesmerized by her gentle nature and distinct appearance.

She was observed regularly by a team of veterinarians and animal caretakers, all of whom noted no major signs of illness or distress in the days leading up to her sudden passing. The sloth had been eating well, engaging in her usual activities, and showing no outward signs of the health problems commonly associated with sloths, such as sluggishness, difficulty eating, or unusual behavior.

It was only after Heidi was found unresponsive in her enclosure that the zoos veterinary team began investigating the cause of death. While an initial examination did not reveal any immediate cause for concern, the suddenness of the death combined with Heidi's status as a rare and highly regarded animal led the zoo to alert authorities and request an external investigation.

Cheyenne Mountain Zoo has stated that they are cooperating fully with authorities and are committed to uncovering the truth behind Heidi's death. In a statement released shortly after the incident, zoo officials said: We are deeply saddened by the loss of Heidi and are taking this matter very seriously. Our team is working with experts to conduct a thorough investigation into the circumstances surrounding her death. The well-being of our animals and their care is our highest priority, and we are committed to transparency throughout this process.

As the investigation into Heidi's death continues, the zoo has pledged to review its current protocols for handling rare and endangered species. The zoos management has also expressed its commitment to improving transparency and communication with the public, as well as cooperating with external agencies to ensure the highest standards of animal welfare moving forward.

For now, Heidi's death remains shrouded in mystery, and the Cheyenne Mountain Zoo and its visitors are left grappling with the loss of a beloved animal. As authorities work to uncover the truth, many hope that the investigation will bring clarity to the circumstances of her untimely passing and help ensure that similar incidents do not occur in the future.

In the meantime, Heidis memory lives on in the hearts of those who had the privilege of seeing her in person, a rare and beautiful animal whose life, though brief, touched the lives of many.
